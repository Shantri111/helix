📋 Introduction

Welcome to Helix Drop! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you play our game.

By playing Helix Drop, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the game.

✅ Simple Summary: We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal information. Your data stays on your device only.
📊 Information We Collect

Helix Drop does NOT collect any personal information.

The game only stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Your best score

📱 This data is stored using your browser's Local Storage — it never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.
🛡️ What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email, or any personal details
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your device information or identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking data
  • Payment or financial information
  • Photos, contacts, or files from your device
🔗 Third-Party Services

Helix Drop uses the following third-party services only to display the game correctly:

  • Google Fonts — for game fonts (Orbitron, Rajdhani). Google may collect font request data per their Privacy Policy.

We do not use any advertising networks, analytics tools, or tracking services.

👶 Children's Privacy

Helix Drop is suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information, the game is safe for children to play.

We comply with applicable children's privacy laws including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

🔐 Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being accessed, disclosed, or misused through our game.

Game progress (score and level) is saved locally on your device and can be cleared by clearing your browser/app data.
